Crafted with the precision of a watchmaker and the soul of an artist, The Obsidian Villa is a masterclass in monolithic modernism. Hidden behind a screen of ancient oaks in the Silverlake Hills, this sanctuary dissolves the boundary between the terrestrial and the atmospheric.

The home features hand-troweled tadelakt walls, sustainable basalt flooring, and a 40-foot cantilevered terrace that offers an uninterrupted frequency of the Los Angeles skyline. Every room is oriented to the solar path, ensuring a natural rhythm of light throughout the seasons.
